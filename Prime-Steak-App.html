Our Story

Best Ingredients and Great Service!

Prime Steak Restaurant was founded in 1995. Our early success was based on the idea that we wanted to make dishes and offer services that make people want to come back for more.

Our goal is to deliver the ultimate dining experience. We have discovered bold flavors and turned them into signature recipes. Our chefs are masters of their craft and are committed to creating memorable experiences for every customer.
