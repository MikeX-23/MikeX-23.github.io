About

Walk to the end of Pristine Beach to take in the beautiful sunsets. With a small population of locals, along with breathtaking, clear-blue waters and immaculate sand beach, Pristine Beach offers a fun and relaxing vacation for families and couples alike.

Location

Residing in the easternmost town in all of Florida, Pristine Beach is located among majestic trees and loaded with beautiful beachfront properties, high-end hotels and sprawling resorts.

Activities

Pristine Beach offers visitors ample activities to enjoy, including beach volleyball, parasailing, a dolphin-watching cruise and a fun fishing excursion.
